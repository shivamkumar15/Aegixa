This Privacy Policy explains how Sailor ("Sailor," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Sailor app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details (name, email, phone number)
  • Profile details (username, profile photo)
  • Emergency contact information you add
  • Device/app diagnostics and notification tokens
  • Location data (with your permission)
  • SOS event metadata and optional safety recordings

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate SOS features and alert workflows
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts
  • Improve app reliability and performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Information

We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, storage, notifications, analytics)
  • Payment platforms (Apple App Store, Google Play, RevenueCat)
  • Emergency contacts you designate for SOS delivery
  • Authorities where legally required

We do not sell your personal information for money.

4. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for service operation, safety, legal, and account purposes.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Update account/profile details in the app
  • Manage permissions in device settings
  • Manage subscriptions in App Store/Google Play settings
  • Request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Children

Sailor is not directed to children under 13 (or local minimum age).

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to appropriate safeguards where required.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the Last Updated date on this page.
